Make Your Verbs Work

Use active and colourful verbs to breathe life and momentum into your writing and inspire your reader to take action. Avoid passive verbs and "-ing" verbs wherever you can. They are weak and removed from the action.

Don'tDo
ThoughtWorks is bringing together top executives from various industries to share...
We invite top executives from various industries to share...
Understand how it was developed by watching this video.
Watch how it was developed.
Learn about participating or presenting at the next event.
Participate or present at the next event.
Your item may have been on a previous radar.
See if your item was on a previous radar.
We use an integrated user experience design and delivery approach.
We integrate user experience design and delivery.

Make your verbs work hard. Use them to help your reader see and feel the action. Write about real people doing real things. Beware of "is" and "was". They often try to make your nouns do the work, instead of making you say what somebody did.

Don'tDo
A common misconception is…
People often think...
But we can’t see any kind of picture, and this is problematic for many reasons.
We get into trouble when we can’t picture the data.
